ABOUT US

ABOUT THE APP

This app was conceived and funded by Pastor How as a free tool for the members of Heart of God Church (Singapore) to conveniently share their faith. It has since been used by many Christians and churches all over the world. If you find this app helpful, feel free to use or share it with others.


ABOUT THE DEVELOPER

Pastor How is a youth pastor at heart, but on weekends, he disguises himself as the Senior Pastor of Heart of God Church. He has a PhD in curiosity and shares his ideas about leading a Youth Church on:

www.PastorHow.com

ABOUT HOGC

Heart of God Church (HOGC) is an independent youth church in Singapore. To find out more about us or our Senior Pastors, Pastor How and Pastor Lia, visit www.heartofgodchurch.org or follow us on social media.

FEATURED MESSAGES & ARTICLES

JOHN

316

GUIDE

SHARING

1.

In the beginning, God created the heavens and Earth.

2.

He also created you and I.

3.

God loves us and wants a relationship with us.

4. John 17:3

- And this is the way to have eternal life - to know you, the only true God, and Jesus Christ, the one you sent to Earth.

5.

Christianity is not a religion but a relationship with God. This relationship brings us true love, joy and peace.

6.

Our relationship with God is broken because of sin.

7.

Sin is as simple as selfishness, lying or lust to as serious as stealing, murder or rebellion towards God.

Romans 3:23

For everyone has sinned; we all fall short of GOd's glorious standards.

8.

Because of sin and our broken relationship with God the father, we will not know our true purpose in life. We are also uncertain of our eternal future.

9. Romans 6:23

- The penalty of sin is death, but the free gift of God is eternal life through Christ Jesus our Lord.

10.

We try to reconcile our relationship with God through many ways.

11.

Some of us try to find happiness by pursuing success, but it does not mend our broken relationship with God.

12.

Some of us try to do good and be good, but relationship with GOd cannot be earned or bought. It is a gift from God.

13.

Some of us try religions or philosophies but God is looking for a loving personal relationship.

14.

All our efforts are not enough to mend this broken relationship. The good news is that God loves us so much that He made the first move.

15. John 3:16

- For God so loved the world that He gave His only begotten Son, that whoever believes in Him should not perish but have everlasting life.

16.

Jesus came on our behalf, and paid the penalty of our sins, by dying on the cross. Our sins can now be erased.

17.

I made a wonderful and life-changing decision to have a relationship with God.

18.

So now, would you like to join me and have your own relationship with God?

19a. [IF YES]

Prayer: Dear Father God, I admit that I am a sinner, and I ask for your forgiveness. I believe that Jesus Christ died for me on the cross and rose again. I confess that Jesus is my Lord and Saviour. Holy Spirit, help me to obey and follow God. Change me from the inside out. In the name of Jesus, amen.

CONGRATULATIONS! You have made the best decision of your life!

19b.[IF NO]

Can I pray a prayer of blessing for you?
